Privacy Policy

Last updated: 7 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the "Telegram to TikTok Bot" application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when it transfers photos from a Telegram channel to a TikTok account on behalf of its sole operator.

1. Who uses this app

The App is a personal automation tool operated by a single individual for their own TikTok account. It is not offered as a service to other users and does not collect data from the public.

2. Information we process

  • Telegram content: Photos and their captions from a Telegram channel the operator is a member of, used solely to repost them.
  • Authentication tokens: TikTok and Telegram access/refresh tokens, stored as encrypted secrets and used only to authenticate API calls.

3. How we use information

Photos are downloaded, resized, temporarily hosted, and submitted to the TikTok Content Posting API for the operator's own account. We do not sell, share, or use any information for advertising or analytics.

4. Data retention

Photos are processed automatically and may be cached on the hosting page used for delivery to TikTok. Authentication tokens are retained only as long as the App is in operation and are rotated automatically.

5. Third-party services

The App interacts with Telegram and TikTok via their official APIs, and uses GitHub for automation and hosting. Their respective privacy policies apply to data processed on their platforms.

6. Contact

For any privacy questions regarding this App, please contact the operator through the TikTok account associated with this application.
